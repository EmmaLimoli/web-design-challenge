City Latitude Vs. Wind Speed

One of the factors we plotted was City Latitude vs. Wind Speed.

We took 500+ cities from all over the world and looked at the wind speed in correlation of the city latitude. The question we wanted to address is: Is there a relationship between these cities proximity to the equator and their wind speed?

The scatter plot shows us the latitude and the wind speed each city exhibits. We can see that a majority of the cities are plotted throughout the scatter plot. There are several outliers both from a wind speed and a latitude perspective. Many of the city plots fall on the northern part of the hemoisphere with a latitude between 0-80. However, that may be due to the cities that were chosen at random. Like cloudiness and humidity, there is not a relationship between the proximity to the equator and wind speed.
