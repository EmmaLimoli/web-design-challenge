Summary: Latitude Vs. X

Welcome to our website!



Our website is dedicated to finding out if there's a correlation between latitude and the weather. We asked whether cities that were closer to the equator would be more humid, hot, windy, or cloudy. We plotted the relationship between the latitude of 500+ cities and these factors.

In the below graphic from (1/05/2017), you can see the relationship between 547 cities from around the world and the maximum temperature they experience. This site is dedicated to finding out the truth between world wide city coordinated and the weather.

We studied the relationships between over 500 cities' latitude coordinates, humidity, maximum temperatures, cloudiness, and wind speed. We came to our conclusion by studying where the city latitudes correspond to those four different factors.

While there are outliers, we found there was more of a relationship between latitude and maximum temperatures in comparison to the other three factors. For a more in-depth analysis, continue through our website.
