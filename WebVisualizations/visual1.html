City Latitude Vs. Max Temps

One of the factors we plotted was City Latitude vs. Max Temperatures.

We took 500+ cities from all over the world and looked at the max temperature in correlation of the city latitude. The question we wanted to address is: Is there a relationship between these cities proximity to the equator and their maximum temperature?

The scatter plot shows us the latitude and the maximum temperature each city exhibits. We can see that a majority of the cities fall around a latitude of 20-40 degrees and a max temperature of 50-100. While there are outliers, we can see a relationship between the latitude of these cities and their maximum temperatures.
