Visualization Comparisons



To provide a thorough analysis, we've compared the four factors that could help determine if there's a correlation between city latitudes and the proximity to the equator. Below, we can see scatter plots for the humidity, cloudiness, wind speed, and the maximum temperature. It's vital to note that these scatter plots are all from 1/05/2017.

As mentioned on maximum temperature, we determined that maximum temperature and latitude have a relationship, but cloudiness, wind speed, and humdity do not. In conclusion, we can state that only maximum temperature increases when closer to the equator for the cities chosen.
