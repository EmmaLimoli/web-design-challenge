City Latitude Vs. Cloudiness

One of the factors we plotted was City Latitude vs. Cloudiness.

We took 500+ cities from all over the world and looked at the cloudiness in correlation to the city latitude. The question we wanted to address is: Is there a relationship between these cities proximity to the equator and their cloudiness?

The scatter plot shows us the latitude and the cloudiness each city exhibits. We can see that a majority of the cities are plotted throughout the graph. Many of the cities are plotted at 0% cloudiness regardless of where it may be located. We can see 0% cloudiness ranges from -50 to 70 degrees latitude. Cloudiness and the proximity to the equator does not seem to have a relationship. There are too many outliers.
